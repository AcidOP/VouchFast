### Invite Clients for Testimonials

<br />

1. **Sign Up** - Create an account on [VouchFast](/auth).
2. **Create a List** - Create a list from [Dashboard](/dashboard/lists).\
   Lists are a categorized testimonials. Organize testimonials based on different projects, clients, or topics.
3. **Add Testimonials** - Select a list and click on 'Share' to copy the link. Share the link with your clients.
